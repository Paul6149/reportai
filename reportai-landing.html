Questions

Everything you need to know.

We already use AgencyAnalytics. Why would we switch?

You don't have to. AgencyAnalytics shows your data — ReportAI explains it. The narrative summary (the paragraph your account manager currently writes manually) is what we automate. Many agencies use both: AgencyAnalytics for internal dashboards, ReportAI for client-facing reports.

How accurate is the AI summary?

Very. It pulls directly from your campaign data, so all numbers are exact. The narrative is generated by Claude (Anthropic's AI), which is specifically good at interpreting marketing data in context. You can always edit before sending — but most users don't need to.

Will my clients know it's AI-generated?

Only if you tell them. Reports are fully white-labeled with your branding, and the narrative reads like it was written by a senior account manager — because it's trained on exactly that kind of writing.

How long does setup actually take?

Under 20 minutes for your first client. You connect Google Ads and GA4 via OAuth (no API keys), upload your logo, and set your delivery schedule. After that, each additional client takes about 3 minutes to add.

Yes — annual plans are available at 2 months free (equivalent to ~17% off monthly pricing). Founding members can also opt for a one-time annual payment at the discounted rate.
